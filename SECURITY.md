# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The MCP Jupyter team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. Thank you for improving the security of our open-source software. We appreciate your efforts and responsible disclosure and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

All security advisories are managed through **[GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/yourusername/mcp-jupyter-server/security/advisories)**.

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.**

### How to Report a Vulnerability

1.  **Go to the `Security` tab** in the repository.
2.  **Click on "New advisory"** to open a new security advisory.
3.  **Provide a descriptive title and a detailed description** of the vulnerability.
4.  **Include as much information as possible**, such as:
    *   The version of the software you are using.
    *   The steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
    *   The potential impact of the vulnerability.
    *   Any suggested mitigations.
5.  **Click "Create draft advisory"** to submit the report.

### Our Commitment

*   We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours**.
*   We will provide a more detailed response within **72 hours**, including our initial assessment of the vulnerability's severity and a timeline for a fix.
*   We will keep you updated on our progress and will notify you when a fix is released.
